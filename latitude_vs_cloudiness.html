Cloudiness vs. Latitude

There is no overall trend for this data, cloudiness seems to vary dramatically at all latitudes. There does seem to be a tendency for more cloudiness in the northern hemisphere than in the southern, perhaps due to the lower temperatures in general leading to more condensation. This would be the result of the fact that this data was pulled in February, when temperatures in the northern hemisphere are colder than temperatures in the southern hemisphere.
